Cookies Recipe

American Cookies

These juicy American Cookies are quite challenging to get just right. Here's how you can do it!

Ingredients

200g flour, 100g sugar, 100g chocolate chips, 100g butter

Steps

  1. 1. Mix all the ingredients
  2. 2. Mold the cookie dough into the shape that you want
  3. 3. Bake the cookies for 40 minutes
  4. 4. Let them cool down and enjoy!
